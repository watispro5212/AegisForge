privacy policy

basically how i handle your data (i don't really want it anyway)

01. what we collect

i only save stuff that the bot actually needs to work. if i don't need it, i don't touch it. simple as that.

User Data

Your Discord user ID, username, and economy/leveling stats. This is so you don't lose your fake money between sessions.

Guild Data

Server IDs and your custom settings (prefixes, logging channels, welcome messages, etc). The bot needs this to know how to behave in your server.

Message Content

Only processed temporarily for leveling and moderation. We don't store your private chats because that's weird and way too much work.

02. how we handle it

your data is stored securely and only ever used to make the bot work. nothing shady going on here.

What We Store

We only store what's absolutely necessary for the bot's features to function:

  • User IDs — to track your economy balance, XP, and levels.
  • Server IDs — to store server-specific configs like prefixes and logs.
  • Message Content — only temporarily processed. Never logged.

Data Security

Your data lives in encrypted Neon Postgres databases. Access is strictly limited to core developers. We don't sell your data because that sounds like too much work anyway.

Your Rights

You can request your data or ask for it to be deleted at any time. Just join the support server and ask. We'll handle it.

Contact Support

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses local storage to remember your preferences. No invasive tracking cookies. We're too lazy for that and also it's kinda creepy.

By using AegisForge, you agree to this policy. If you don't like it, you can always kick the bot. No hard feelings.

Questions? Join the support server.
