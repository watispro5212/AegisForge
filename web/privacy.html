Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

AegisForge collects minimal data necessary to function securely and reliably. This includes:

  • Server IDs & Configuration: Guild configurations, role settings, and channel preferences.
  • User IDs: When executing moderation commands or receiving warnings, User IDs are stored for audit logs.
  • Message Metadata: For message caching and purging, temporary message IDs may be stored in memory.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this data strictly to provide AegisForge's intended functionality, such as keeping track of moderation warnings, executing timed reminders, and managing auto-roles. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

3. Data Retention

Moderation logs and user warnings are kept indefinitely for audit purposes unless explicitly purged by a server administrator. Reminders are deleted immediately upon successful execution.

4. Your Rights

Server administrators may request data deletion for their guild at any time by removing the bot or contacting our support team in the official AegisForge support server.
