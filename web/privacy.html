privacy policy

basically how i handle your data (i don't really want it anyway)

01. what i collect

i only save stuff that the bot actually needs to work. if i don't need it, i don't touch it. simple as that.

user data

your discord user id, username, and whatever economy/leveling stats you have. this is so you don't lose your fake money.

guild data

server ids and your custom settings (prefixes, logging channels, etc). i need this so the bot knows how to behave in your server.

message content

i only look at messages to check for bad words (automod) or commands. i don't store your private chats because that's weird and too much work.

02. why i collect it

mostly so the bot functions. economy needs balances, leveling needs xp, and moderation needs to know who to ban. i'm not selling this to anyone, i don't even know how to do that lol.

03. third parties

the bot runs on some services like neon (database) and discord obviously. they might see some technical stuff but they have their own policies. go read those if you're bored.

04. data deletion

if you want your data gone, just kick the bot or join the support server and ask. i'll probably do it whenever i wake up from my nap.

got questions? just join the support server or whatever.
