About AegisForge

aegisforge is just a discord bot i made using rust. it does moderation and some other stuff. i made it because i wanted a bot that works fast and doesn't crash all the time.

other bots are slow and have too much junk. i made this one simple. it uses a database so it saves your stuff. it works for small servers or big ones, doesn't really matter.

i'll probably add more stuff later when i feel like it. for now it just works i guess.
