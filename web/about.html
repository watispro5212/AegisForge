About AegisForge

AegisForge is a next-generation Discord moderation and utility bot built entirely from the ground up in Rust. Designed for communities that demand maximum performance, zero downtime, and a highly polished interface.

Traditional bots are often sluggish, bloated with unnecessary features, and prone to going offline. We set out to change that. By leveraging Rust's fearless concurrency and a cutting-edge serverless database architecture via Neon Postgres, AegisForge operates at the speed of metal.

Whether you're managing a 10-person private group or a 100,000-member community hub, AegisForge scales effortlessly to meet your needs without compromising on response times or reliability.
