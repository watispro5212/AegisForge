Economy System v4.1

the economy engine

A full server economy with wallets, banking, shop purchases, inventory, gambling, fishing, hunting, and crime. Earn, buy, collect, bank, and climb the leaderboard.

💵 Earning Money

Multiple income streams with different cooldowns and risk levels. Balances are isolated per server — every guild has its own economy.

/economy daily
⏱ 24h cooldown
Claim your daily reward of $500. Resets every 24 hours — check in daily to stay competitive.
/economy work
⏱ 30min cooldown
Put in the hours for a random payout of $50–$200. The most reliable grind in the forge.
/economy fish
⏱ 5min cooldown
Cast your line in the Forge Lake. Catch anything from a $20 carp to a $5,000 legendary golden fish.
/economy hunt
⏱ 10min cooldown
Venture into the wild. Track prey from a $30 rabbit all the way to a $15,000 dragon (0.5% chance).
/economy crime
⏱ 1h cooldown
High-risk, high-reward. Earn $500–$2,000 on success — or pay a $400 fine if you get caught.
/economy beg
No cooldown
Ask a stranger for spare change. Earns $0–$50 — sometimes nothing. Last resort for the truly broke.
/economy search
No cooldown
Rummage through random locations — sofas, bins, parks — to find $10–$150 in loose change.

🏦 Wallet & Bank

Your money lives in two places: your wallet (spendable, robbable) and your bank (safe, locked away). Move funds between them at any time.

/economy balance
Check your wallet, bank, and total net worth. You can also view another user's balance.
/economy deposit <amount>
Move money from your wallet into the bank for safekeeping. Banked funds cannot be stolen.
/economy withdraw <amount>
Pull funds out of the bank back into your wallet when you need to spend or gamble.
/economy pay <user> <amount>
Send money from your wallet directly to another user. Can't pay what you don't have.
💡 Pro tip: Always bank your earnings after a big win. Only your wallet balance is at risk from /economy rob — money in the bank is completely safe.

🥷 Robbery

Take a risk and attempt to steal from another user's wallet. Success and failure both have consequences.

/economy rob <user>
Attempt to steal 10%–50% of a user's wallet. 40% success rate — get caught and pay a $200 fine. Target needs at least $100.
⚠️ Risk warning: Failed robberies cost you $200 from your wallet. Bank your money first — only wallet funds are at risk.

🛒 Global Shop

The AegisForge v4.1 shop is a global catalog shared across every server. It is auto-rendered from the bot's current shop list, so new profile items, collectibles, boosts, utility items, community tags, and limited drops appear in one place.

/economy shop
Browse the full global catalog grouped by category. Current categories include Profile, Cosmetic, Collectible, Boost, Utility, Community, and Limited.
/economy shop category:limited
Filter the catalog by category when the list gets large.
/economy buy <item> <quantity>
Buy items from the global catalog using wallet funds. Item IDs like hyperforge_core and exact item names both work.
/economy inventory
View purchased shop items, quantities, item IDs, and rarity labels for yourself or another user.
/economy profile
Open a compact v4.1 snapshot with wallet, bank, net worth, server rank, global rank, and owned item count.
🧾 Catalog note: The catalog is global, while inventories are saved per server economy. Staff can add new shop entries in the bot's catalog list and the command will automatically display the updated inventory after deployment.
