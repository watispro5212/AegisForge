Economy System

The Economy Engine

A fully persistent, per-server economy backed by Neon PostgreSQL. Earn, bank, gamble, and climb the leaderboard — your progress is saved across every session.

💵 Earning Money

Build your fortune using these income commands. Balances are isolated per server — every guild has its own economy.

/economy daily
⏱ 24h cooldown
Claim your daily reward of $500. Miss a day and the streak resets, so check in every 24 hours.
/economy work
⏱ 30min cooldown
Put in the hours for a random payout of $50–$200. The most reliable grind in the Forge.
/economy beg
No cooldown
Ask a stranger for spare change. Earns $0–$50 — sometimes nothing. A last resort for the truly broke.
/economy search
No cooldown
Rummage through random locations — sofas, bins, parks — to find $10–$150 in loose change.

🏦 Wallet & Bank

Your money lives in two places: your wallet (spendable, robbable) and your bank (safe, locked away). Move funds between them at any time.

/economy balance
Check your wallet, bank, and total net worth. You can also view another user's balance.
/economy deposit <amount>
Move money from your wallet into the bank for safekeeping. Banked funds cannot be stolen.
/economy withdraw <amount>
Pull funds out of the bank back into your wallet when you need to spend or gamble.
/economy pay <user> <amount>
Send money from your wallet directly to another user. Can't pay what you don't have.
💡 Pro tip: Always bank your earnings after a big win. Only your wallet balance is at risk from /economy rob — money in the bank is completely safe.

🥷 Robbery

Take a risk and attempt to steal from another user's wallet. Success and failure both have consequences.

/economy rob <user>
Attempt to steal 10%–50% of a user's wallet balance. You need a 40% success rate — get caught and pay a $200 fine. Target must have at least $100 in their wallet.
⚠️ Risk warning: Failed robberies cost you $200 directly from your wallet. Bank your money before getting robbed — only wallet funds are at risk.

🏆 Leaderboards

/economy global_leaderboard
View the top 10 wealthiest players across all servers in the AegisForge network.
