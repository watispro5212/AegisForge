# 🎰 AegisForge: The Gambler's Guide

Welcome to the **Eternal Forge** economy. Whether you're a high-stakes gambler or a cautious saver, here is everything you need to know about the "gambling stuff" and how to maximize your wealth.

## 1. 🎰 The Slot Machine (`/economy slots`)

We have heavily buffed the slot machine to be more rewarding for players.

### **The Odds**

The machine uses 5 symbols: 🍒, 🍋, 🍇, 💎, and ⭐.

- **Triple Match (3 identical)**: ~4% chance.
- **Double Match (2 identical)**: ~48% chance.
- **Diamond Consolation (At least one 💎)**: ~30% additional chance.
- **Overall Winning Chance**: **~82%** (One of the highest in Discord history!)


### **The Multipliers**


- **💎💎💎 (Mega Jackpot)**: **10x** your bet.
- **✨ Triple Match**: **5x** your bet.
- **🍀 Double Match**: **2x** your bet.
- **💎 Diamond Bonus**: **1.5x** your bet (Consolation).

## 2. 🥷 Robbery Mechanics (`/economy rob`)

High risk, high reward. Here’s how it works:

- **Success Rate**: **40%**.
- **Payout**: If successful, you steal between **10% and 50%** of the target's current **Wallet Balance**.
- **Risk**: If you fail, you gain nothing. (Future updates may add fines for failed robberies!)
- **Target Limit**: You cannot rob users who have less than **$100** in their wallet.

---

## 3. 🏦 Banking vs. Wallet

Understanding where your money is kept is crucial for security.

- **Wallet**: This is where your money goes when you `work` or `beg`. Money in your wallet **can be stolen** by other users using `/economy rob`.
- **Bank**: Use `/economy deposit` to move money to your bank. Money in the bank is **100% safe** and cannot be robbed.
- **Withdraw**: Use `/economy withdraw` when you need to take money out to gamble or pay someone.

---

## 4. 📈 How to Get Rich

1. **Consistency**: Use `/economy daily` every 24 hours for a guaranteed boost.
2. **Hard Work**: Use `/economy work` regularly.
3. **Strategic Saving**: Keep only what you need to gamble in your wallet. **Deposit the rest!**
4. **The Diamond Strategy**: Since landing even one 💎 now gives a 1.5x return, playing slots is a viable long-term growth strategy.
